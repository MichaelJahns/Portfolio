About Me

I am a well-traveled cool guy who has worn alot of hats. I am college graduate from Washington State University, degree in Biology minor Chemistry. During my time as a Peace Corps Volunteer I served my community as a Senior High Chemistry Teacher, Elementary Phonics Instructor/Teacher Trainer, and Student Affairs Liason. While in Liberia I explored their burdened Medical Infracstructure and worked to change Peace Corps approach to Malaria; piloted and analyzed two nationwide surveys, served as the commitees chair, and was invited to STOMP, a malaria prevention think tank in Senegal. Currently I am enrolled in Code Fellows, gaining the skills and knowledge I'll need to do brave and innovative projects for the betterment of peoples lifes.
